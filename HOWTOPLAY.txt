### Playing the game

Easy way:
1. Visit this link:
   http://johnearnest.github.io/Octo/index.html?gist=a8a227e707cd138ba258

Harder way:
1. Open Octo
   http://johnearnest.github.io/Octo/
2. Change to 1000Cycles/Frame
3. Click Options menu and enable XO-Chip
4. Paste contents of trucksim.8o into the codebox and press run

### Controls

  W = Steer left
  S = Steer right
  E = Select menu option
